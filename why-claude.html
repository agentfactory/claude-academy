Why Claude? An Honest Look at AI Platforms

Picking an AI platform is a foundation decision for your personal operating system. Claude prioritizes persistent context, not just chat responses.

The Foundation Decision
Choosing an AI platform is like choosing your operating system, not your favorite app. You build everything on top of it. Switching later costs time, context, and momentum. Claude is the best foundation because it treats every conversation as part of a larger system you are building over time.
What Sets Claude Apart

Persistent Context

Projects, memory, and custom instructions that carry across every conversation. No more re-explaining who you are.

Long-Form Thinking

Handles complex reasoning, long documents, and nuanced tasks where quick answers are not enough.

Extensibility

Connectors, plugins, MCPs, and Code mode. Claude grows with your skill level instead of boxing you in.

Platform vs. Tool
Platform Mindset

Claude as Your OS

You build workflows, store context, and extend capabilities over time. Every session adds value to the next one.

  • Persistent memory across sessions
  • Projects with custom instructions
  • Skills, connectors, and MCPs
  • Desktop app with file access

AI as a Search Bar

You ask one-off questions. Every conversation starts from scratch. No compounding value over time.

  • No memory between sessions

  • Copy and paste everything manually
  • Stuck in the browser tab
